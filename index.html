About Us

Get ready for some Folky, Spiritual and Funny songs of love and wonder; Something Elfse is a Swedish/Slovenian duo of singer-songwriters Albin and Danaya, and together they create a blend of Folk, Triphop, and Meditation - always with a spiritual message of hope and happiness. Their music has the emotional depth of the ocean, yet reaches spiritual heights of the mountain tops; it channels the whimsical world of the fairies of the woods, and the serene stillness of the celestial realms and the mystics of old.
